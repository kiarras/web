OCSA Geophysical company

In more than 30 years of experience as a company specializing in geophysical services, our identity has always been quality. And for this commitment we are now a certified company in UNE ISO-9001 and UNE ISO-14001.

With a highly qualified staff, our work seeks to successfully meet the goals set by our clients.

There are already more than 1000 projects carried out with companies of reputed prestige both Spanish and international.

Currently offering geotechnical and I+D+i consulting services, we have a MARCO agreement with the company CONTROL Y ESTUDIOS. And internationally agreements in Colombia (Ulloa y Diez Ltd.) and Chile (Paine Geotécnia.).

Since 2015 we are the representatives in Spain of Kinemetrics, company specializing in seismic monitoring instruments. Used in sensitive buildings such as bridges, power plants, or nuclear power plants.

In the year 2018 we have created a collaboration agreement with OSAIMI ENGINEERING OFFICE, in Saudi Arabia. As consultants of its Non Destructive Testing department, for geophysical projects

In the development and improvement of our skills in applications from geophysics to geothermal , our R&D team has designed and manufactured an Accelerated Weight Drop (AWD) system with sufficient capacity for projects where it is necessary to reach great depths. Receiving a clearer seismic signal response.
